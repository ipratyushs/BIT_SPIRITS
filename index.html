About

Thrill, joy and ecstasy are words that are synonymous with BITXTREME, the Annual Cultural Fest of
Birla Institute of Technology, Noida.


BIT XTREME is a two day extravaganza; a celebration of sorts; a dense, fun-filled embodiment of the energy and conviviality which characterizes college life; a festival oozing with fun, vibrancy and entertainment. It is a product of dreams ambition and tireless hard work, a true testimony to the indomitable spirit, liveliness and sheer energy of the youth.

Rules for Tech Quiz

Round 1 Online MCQ
General technical knowledge based questions.
No negative marking.
Some programming based questions.
Round 2 Buzzer Round
Rapid fire round.
Simple technical questions to be answered as quickly as possible.
Certificates for all participants in both events.

Rules for Chai Par Charcha

  • 3 participants for the motion .
  • 3 participants against the motion .
  • 2 minutes to every candidate for every round.
  • Negative marking for crossing the time limit .
  • Extra points on question asking within time.
  • Need to respond to audience questions.
  • Award: certificates for all participants.
  • Note: In case of extra participants there will be a pre qualification round.

    • Rules for Best Out of Waste

  • Team event(2-3 members)
  • Materials will be provided.
  • Stationary will be yours.
  • Preparation time-1hr
  • Presentation time-5 minutes

    • Rules for Lan Gaming

  • Gather for the 5V5 Competetive.
  • Teams will be competing on the map DUST 2.
  • Registration fee per team is 150Rs.
  • Contestants are allowed to bring their own equipment.
  • However, systems will be provided by the organisers.
  • Prizes only for the winning team.

    • Rules for Mock Interview

    Round 1 App God

  • Conjure up an innovative app idea, draw up a general UI and explain the app to the interviewer.
  • Round 2 Interview

  • Get the experience of being in a real interview, answer technical questions, get helpful tips and prepare for placement interviews.

    • Rules for Laughter Challenge

  • Q/A round to be answered in funny way by participant.
  • Mimic a movie scene.
  • Stand-up comedy of 3-4 mins.

    • Rules for Mr. & Mrs. BIT NOIDA

  • Shortlisted candidates based on given criteria will be again filtered by the jury(panel of teachers)
  • Top 3 finalists(3Males & 3Females) will qualify for final rounds on stage.

    • Rules for JAM

  • EVERY PARTICIPANT WILL COMPETE AGAINST SOME OTHER PARTICIPANTS
    IN A GIVEN TASK.
  • THE PERSON WHO WINS THAT TASK QUALIFIES FOR THE NEXT ROUND.
  • THE DECISION OF THE JUDGES/ORGANISERS WILL BE FINAL.

    • Rules for Fab AD for the MAD

    Round 1 :

  • (Selection and elimination round ) advertisement teaser
  • ROUND 2:
  • Time limit = 3 min ( 1min ad + 2 min preparation time)
  • Team size = 4 members
  • Team will have to make a radio jingle as well as TV commercial AD ( should be appealing to the audience)
  • Topic will be provided along with a kit on the spot to present your AD .
  • The AD prepared must not contain any obscene words, any subject that hurt the feelings of a culture ( language/ religion/ region)
  • Judging will be done on teams originality, humor , stage presentation and usage of props.
  • Participants have to make use of the material given in the kit.
  • Use of any other material would lead to disqualification of team
  • ROUND 3:
  • Mentos round - Deemag ki batti jalao ( Question answer round)

    • Rules for Rangoli

  • Team participation (2-3 participants)
  • Material will not be provided
  • Time duration: 1 hour
  • Theme: Halloween

    • Rules for Singing

  • Duets and solo's both are allowed.
  • Time limit ( 4 mim max )
  • No electronic instruments will be provided manage on your own.
  • Lyrics should be sung correctly.
  • If you want to bring your instrumental music bring it with you in a USB.

    • Rules for Antakshari

  • A TEAM OF 3 MEMBERS MUST REGISTER BEFORE 23rd MARCH
  • RULES AND REGULATIONS WILL BE DISCLOSED AT THE TIME OF THE EVENT.

    • Rules for Face Painting

  • A team of 2 people is allowed where one will be drawing and the other member will work as a model.
  • Participants are required to bring their own material but make sure the colours used are apt to apply on face.
  • Acrylic colours are not allowed neither any type of stencils.
  • Use of paint brush and sponge is only permitted. Glitter can also be used.
  • No help or assistant is permitted.
  • Total time to complete the task will be 45 mins.
  • The topic for the competition is "GOTHIC". Try to use orange and black colour prominently.
  • Judges decision will be final.

    • Rules for Dramebaaz

  • Participation :individual or a team of 2.
  • Rounds:Qualification round and final round .
  • Act should not be more than 5 minutes in first round and 10 minutes in second round .
  • Acts related to the fest theme and social issue will be appreciated .
  • No obsence action or language should be used during the act.
  • Use of props should be informed before hand .
  • Judges decision will be final and outstanding .

    • Rules for Chance Pe Dance

  • There will be 2 rounds:
  • 1st Round: Solo dance
  • 2nd Round: Dancing with a prop.(for those selected in the 1st round,prop will be given to them)
  • Marks will be awarded on basis of prop usage
  • The decision of the judges shall be binding.

    • Rules for Beg Borrow Steal

  • A Team will comprise of upto 3 players.
  • All the teams will be provided with a list of items.
  • Team members will have to complete the task within a time limit of 30 minutes.
  • Team can either beg, borrow or steal the items that are enlisted and provided to them.
  • Teams will have to get themselves registered before the commencement of the event.
  • After the completion of the task the teams are required to report to the coordinators for evaluation.
  • Teams will be judged on first come, first serve basis and also the items they were able to collect.
  • Decision of the judges is final and binding in case of any conflict.
